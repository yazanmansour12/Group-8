F-35 Lightning II

The F-35 is a modern American stealth fighter jet made by Lockheed Martin. It is designed for air-to-air and air-to-ground missions and is known for its advanced technology, speed, and low visibility to radar.

Main Features

  • Stealth technology
  • High speed and maneuverability
  • Advanced sensors and systems
  • Used by many countries

Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is working with the United States to acquire the F-35 in the future.
